Idea Concept

Through observations and surveys, many camps in temasek polytechnic contained a certain element of wet games included in its schedule. The majority of students played the wet games and these caused many students to be wet and time needed to be set aside for showering and changing. Hence, with M@GIC shirt, students will no longer need to head to the toilet to change immediately and all they need would be a towel to dry their hair and remaining water.

Unique Selling Point

Opposed to the current shirts available out there in the market, M@GIC provides a water-resistant shirt which:

  • Increases efficiency
  • Increases time management

Value Proposition

Teachers and other individuals who wants students to keep dry and not fall seek can now be reassured that with this M@GIC chemical, it can be applied to almost all types of fabrics:

Jeans

Jackets

Carpets
