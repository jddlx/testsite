Framework, Design, & Methodology

Shirts/Jeans/Carpets etc.

The chemical can be applied to almost all types of fabrics that the polymers can go through. The chemical extends the life of products and keep them looking newer longer. These long-chain fluorinated polymers often contain residual raw materials and trace levels of long-chain acids as impurities. The residual raw materials and the product themselves may degrade in the environment to form long-chain PFAAs.

Application of Chemical

The chemical should be added into a washing machine and fabrics that wants to be water resistant can be thrown into the washing machine.
